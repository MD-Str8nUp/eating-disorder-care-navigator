Free independent navigation aid

When an eating disorder needs medical care, not just more referrals.

If you or someone you love or care for may be medically unsafe, very underweight, losing weight quickly, or being sent home without a clear eating disorder care plan, this pathway tool can help you work out what to ask and who to contact.

  • Urgent safety prompts
  • BMI risk context and government BMI calculator link
  • Questions to ask before discharge
  • Coordinator/contact pathways where public information is available

Not medical advice. Not an official NEDC, PHN or hospital website.
