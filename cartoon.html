ED Care Navigator cartoon.

A short cartoon for families, carers and clinicians about when an eating disorder may need medical assessment or coordinated pathway support, not just another referral.

Download the cartoon (MP4) Open the navigator

Video transcript

When someone may be medically unsafe, the next step can be medical assessment. Very low weight, fainting, rapid weight loss, or not eating and drinking need attention.

A clear pathway helps connect the pieces: GP, hospital, mental health and eating disorder support. It can help families and clinicians ask the right discharge and follow-up questions.

Care coordinators can help families and clinicians work out who to contact next.
