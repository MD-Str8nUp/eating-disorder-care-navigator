About text:
Eating Disorder Care Navigator Australia is an independent information and pathway navigation page for people, families, carers and professionals trying to work out the next step when an eating disorder may be medically unsafe or needs coordinated care.

The linked website includes urgent safety prompts, BMI risk context, questions to ask before leaving hospital, and public pathway/contact information where available.

This page and website do not provide medical advice and are not official NEDC, PHN, hospital or government resources. In an emergency, call 000 or attend the nearest Emergency Department.
