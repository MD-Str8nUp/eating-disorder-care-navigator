I am sharing a free Australian eating disorder care navigation website that may be useful for hospital, emergency, discharge planning and social work teams supporting families where an eating disorder may be medically unsafe or where follow-up is unclear.

Website:
https://eating-disorder-care-navigator.vercel.app

The site includes plain-English prompts families can use before leaving hospital, including:

- has the eating disorder medical risk been assessed, not just today's presenting issue?
- what should we do tonight?
- what symptoms mean return to Emergency?
- who is responsible for follow-up?
- is there a written plan connecting the GP, hospital, mental health and specialist supports?

It is not medical advice and is not an official NEDC, PHN, hospital or government resource. It is intended as an independent navigation aid for families and professionals when care feels fragmented.

If the wording or pathway information should be corrected for your service, I would be grateful for any feedback.
