I am sharing a free Australian eating disorder care navigation website that may be useful for {{organisation}}, particularly for GPs, practice nurses, reception teams and families trying to work out the next step when an eating disorder may be medically unsafe.

Website:
https://eating-disorder-care-navigator.vercel.app

The site is designed to help people understand when urgent medical assessment may be needed, how very low BMI and rapid weight loss can affect risk, what families can ask before leaving hospital, and how care coordination may help connect the GP, hospital, mental health and specialist supports.

It is not medical advice, and it is not an official NEDC, PHN, hospital or government website. It is intended as a practical navigation aid for families, carers and clinicians who are trying to avoid disconnected referrals and no clear plan.

If useful, please consider sharing it with staff or keeping it available for patients and carers who need a clearer pathway.
