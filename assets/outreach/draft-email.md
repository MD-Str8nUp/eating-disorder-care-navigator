Subject: Practical eating disorder care pathway resource for NT clinicians and practices

Dear Practice Manager / Clinical Team,

I am sharing a free Australian eating disorder care navigation website that may be useful for GPs, practice nurses, hospitals, urgent care clinics, Aboriginal health services, mental health teams and families supporting someone who may be medically unsafe.

Website:
https://eating-disorder-care-navigator.vercel.app/

The site is designed to help people understand when an eating disorder may need urgent medical assessment, how BMI and rapid weight loss can affect risk, what questions to ask before discharge, and how eating disorder care coordinators may help connect GP, hospital, mental health and specialist supports.

It is not medical advice, and it is not an official NEDC, PHN or hospital website. It is intended as a practical navigation aid for families and clinicians who are trying to work out the right next step.

If this is useful, please consider sharing it with staff or keeping it available for patients and carers who need a clearer pathway.
